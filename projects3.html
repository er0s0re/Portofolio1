About MyList Project

A simple solution for complex problems.

The MyList Odoo project was born from the need for a simple yet powerful task management tool, without leaving the Odoo ecosystem. Many To-Do List apps exist, but often lack proper integration with the ERP system already in use. This module allows users to create, organize, assign, and track personal or team tasks directly from their Odoo Dashboard. The goal is to reduce switching between apps, simplify workflows, and ensure all project or operational tasks are recorded in one place.

Key Features Offered

Designed for ease and efficiency.

Full Integration with Odoo

No need to open another app. Create and manage your tasks directly from the familiar Odoo environment. Connects with other modules like Projects, CRM, or Sales.

  • Quick access from Odoo's main menu.
  • Link tasks to contacts or documents.
  • Consistent look and feel with Odoo UI.

Collaborative Task Management

Delegate tasks to team members, set deadlines, and monitor progress in real-time. Everyone knows what needs to be done.

  • Assign tasks to other users.
  • Set priorities (Low, Medium, High).
  • Leave comments or notes on each task.
